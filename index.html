In my opinion, copywrite is something you should never do. Copywritting something can get you in a lot of trouble

When you copywrite something, you can get arrested and put in jail/juvy for a long time.

Rather than copywritting something, read whatever it is you were GOING to copywrite and put it into your own words.

If it is something that you can't/ don't know how to rephrase, make it a quote and site where and who it is from.

For example, say you were reading about koalas and wanted to write and essay about them.

You found information on them that you don't want to change because it is worded perfectly.
